When you need armed or unarmed protection for yourself, your property, or your business, you want a company that has what it takes to keep you and the things that matter to you safe and protected. At Homeland Intelligence and Protective Services, LLC, we promise to share our strength, provide you with reliable security services, and show you we have integrity in everything we do. We can meet your security and patrol service needs.

Homeland Intelligence and Protection Services, LLC, employs the top protection officers. Many of our protection officers are active duty or retired law enforcement personnel. Our armed protection officers are registered and in compliance with the Pennsylvania State Police Lethal Weapons Training Act, Florida Department of Arigriculture and have met all State requirements. In addition to the state requirements, all officers go through our in house training program and client specific training.
